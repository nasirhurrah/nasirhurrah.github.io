Research

Research Interests

Internet of Things (IoT) & Visual-IoT Security

Protection of Visual-IoT (VIoT) data against cyber-attacks, secure sensor networks, lightweight cryptographic protocols, and privacy-preserving transmission of visual/industrial data over resource-constrained IoT devices.

Digital Signal Processing & Embedded Systems

Design and implementation of DSP algorithms on embedded platforms, real-time signal acquisition and processing, low-power architectures, and hardware–software co-design for industrial and biomedical applications.

Industrial Image Processing & Watermarking

Industrial forgery detection, dual watermarking frameworks for authentication and tamper localization, quality inspection using computer vision, and robust methods for harsh industrial environments.

Machine Learning for Healthcare & Industry 4.0

Machine learning and deep learning for disease detection, e-healthcare security, predictive maintenance, and intelligent monitoring in Industry 4.0 settings.

Information Security & Cryptography

Design of secure data transmission frameworks, cryptographic primitives for constrained devices, and security solutions for e-healthcare and smart manufacturing systems.

Current Projects

  • Security Frameworks for Visual-IoT Systems — Developing lightweight, robust schemes to protect visual sensor data from tampering, interception, and forgery in Industrial IoT deployments.
  • Robust Watermarking for Industrial Image Authentication — Designing dual watermarking techniques for authentication and tamper localization in critical industrial inspection images.
