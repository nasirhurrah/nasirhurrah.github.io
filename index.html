About

"I am currently working as Assistant Professor at University of Kashmir. I recently completed my three year Postdoc in Oct. 2024 at Aligarh Muslim University in the area: Protection of Visual-IoT (VIoT) data against Cyber Attacks and completed my Ph.D. at the University of Kashmir in 2020. I have rich experience of teaching/research in the areas of Signal Processing, Internet of Things (IoT), Embedded Systems, Information Security, Image processing, communication engineering, and other engineering courses, and in various academic and professional service activities. I have done a significant work in these fields and published my work in various SCI journals of IEEE, Elsevier and Springer. Besides I have worked as an editor of a book published in Springer Nature and supervised many projects done using MATLAB, Python and Arduino."
