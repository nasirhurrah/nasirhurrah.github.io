About

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics & IT, University of Kashmir, Srinagar, India. I recently completed a three-year Post-Doctoral Fellowship in October 2024 at Aligarh Muslim University in the area of protection of Visual-IoT (VIoT) data against cyber attacks, and I obtained my Ph.D. from the University of Kashmir in 2020.

I have rich experience of teaching and research in signal processing, Internet of Things (IoT), embedded systems, information security, image processing, communication engineering, and other core electronics and IT courses. I have published my work in leading SCI-indexed journals of IEEE, Elsevier and Springer, edited a book with Springer Nature, and supervised several projects using MATLAB, Python and Arduino.

Research Snapshot

My current research is focused on the following broad themes:

  • Secure and reliable Internet of Things (IoT) and Visual-IoT systems
  • Digital signal processing and embedded system architectures
  • Industrial image authentication, watermarking and forgery detection
  • Machine learning for disease detection and e-Healthcare security
  • Image processing, cryptography and information security

For more details, see the Research and Publications pages.
